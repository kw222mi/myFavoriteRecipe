Biscotti - with orange and almond

These italian almond crusts are one of my favorite things for fika. The name biscotti comes from the words bis = two times, cotto = baked I got the recipe from a bakery so you may have to divide it in half or even less.

Ingredients:
  • 274 g butter
  • 1041 g sugar
  • 555 g egg
  • 45 g vanilla sugar
  • 1292 g flour
  • 23 g cinnamon
  • 35 g baking Soda
  • 21 g salt
  • 1100 g nuts
  • 75 g orange zest
  1. Mix sugar and butter
  2. Add eggs, one at a time
  3. Add the sifted dry ingredients and mix lightly
  4. Add nuts and orange zest and mix the dough until completely mixed
  5. Weigh out 1000g, place on a baking sheet and flatten - approx. 2.5 cm high
  6. Bake at 165 degrees for 35-40 minutes. The cookies should be firm.
  7. When they have cooled, cut them open and bake again at 120 degrees until they are completely dry.
